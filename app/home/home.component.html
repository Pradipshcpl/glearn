Vehicle Park Count Systems

Vehicle Counting Overview

Vehicle counting is becoming a common requirement for parking lot owners regardless of their size. It is a great convenience for contractors to be aware of the number of vehicles enter a car park or venture into another section of the car park. Traffic monitoring on roads is also becoming commonplace as highway authorities and council transport departments strive to reduce congestion.

For vehicles to be counted present in parking lot, there needs to be a security who will keep record of them. As a vehicle checkIn, security will update visitor's details in gparking app which later gives count to contractor. In Gparking application, security has to update visitor's details while checkIn as well as checkOut. Through this we can maintain a count of vehicles entering and exiting each area. It then computes the date as necessary to provide the required display information to contractor.

Typically, the count system will provide comprehensive on-screen information to the management.

Simple Space / Full Display

Many counting systems simply need to keep a record of the vehicles entering and leaving a car park. When the car park is empty the count value is set to zero. As each vehicle enters the car park the count is incremented in vehicles present in lot now. As each vehicle leaves the car park the count is incremented in vehicles out of lot now. On this basis we can keep a record of vehicles checkedin and checkedout every day.
